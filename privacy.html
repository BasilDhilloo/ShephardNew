At its core, privacy is about control.

And at Lucky Shepherd, we’re firm believers that you should have control over your own data and know exactly how it’s being used. By using this website, you implicitly grant us and our Partners the ability to access, use, and store the resulting data to give you the fully optimized user experience you are seeking. Partners are trusted third parties who perform functions and provide services to us, including site hosting and maintenance, customer relationship, database storage and management, advertising, and direct marketing campaigns. We ensure that each of these Partners executes a contract with Lucky Shepherd obligating them to securely store your information and respect your privacy.
